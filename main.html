Marvel movies have become a global phenomenon, captivating audiences with their blend of action, emotion, and unforgettable characters. Beginning with *Iron Man* in 2008, the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) has evolved into a vast interconnected world of superheroes, including iconic figures like Captain America, Spider-Man, and Thor. With each new film, Marvel introduces high-stakes battles, thrilling adventures, and deep character development. The MCU's ability to weave complex stories and bring beloved comic book characters to life has earned it critical acclaim and commercial success, making it one of the most influential movie franchises of all time.
